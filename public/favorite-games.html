My Favorite Video Games

By José María Chinchilla Talbott

1. Golden Sun (GBA) and Golden Sun: The Lost Age (GBA)

This was my first RPG. Thanks to it, role-playing games are my favorite genre.

2. Kingdom Hearts II (PS2)

Still, the gameplay of this game is second to none.

3. Metroid Zero Mission (GBA)

This game made me fall in love with the Metroid franchise. I still play this game at least once a year.

4. Pokemon Crystal (GBC)

Not only the best Pokemon game ever made, it is also one of the best games ever made, period.

5. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild (Switch)

Probably the best Zelda game ever made, even with Tears of the Kingdom in the picture.
