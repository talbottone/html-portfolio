My Top 3 Movies of all Time

According to José Talbott

Walk Hard: The Dewey Cox Story

I quote this movie at least once every day. It isn't a critically acclaimed movie by any means, but
I love every second of it.

Toy Story and Toy Story 2

I cheated a little bit and added two movies in one category, but both of these are my childhood. Even
now I still imagine now and then that my Lego figures take life and move.

Dunkirk

What I love most about this film is its masterful score. Full of emotion in every scene. It truly evokes
the sinking feeling of uncertainty that war is riddled with.
